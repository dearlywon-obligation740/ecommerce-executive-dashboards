## 📄 About ecommerce-executive-dashboards

This application helps you turn raw e-commerce data into clear and useful reports. It takes your sales and customer data, cleans it automatically, and creates Excel dashboards with easy controls like slicers. The dashboards use Excel features such as CUBEVALUE and dark-themed charts, making the data easier to understand and explore.  

You do not need to know Python or Excel coding. This tool works behind the scenes and delivers final reports for decision-making.

## 📊 How to Use the Application

### Step 1: Prepare Your Data  
You need your e-commerce sales or customer data in Excel or CSV format. It should include columns like:  
- Date of sale  
- Product name or ID  
- Quantity sold  
- Price or revenue  
- Customer region or segment  

### Step 2: Load Your Data  
Open the application. You will see an option to upload or select your data file.

Click **Browse**, find your file, and select it.

The app will clean and organize the data automatically. This includes fixing dates, removing duplicates, and standardizing product names.

### Step 3: Generate Dashboards  
After the data is ready, click **Create Dashboards**.

The app builds Excel dashboards that update instantly based on your latest data.

### Step 4: Explore Your Data  
Open the Excel dashboards created by the app.

Use the slicers (clickable filters on the side) to choose dates, products, or regions.

The charts and tables will change to show only the data you want.

You can save these Excel files and share them with your team or executives.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Tips

- If the dashboard does not show data, check that your Excel version supports Power Pivot and CUBEVALUE.  
- Make sure your data columns match expected names: Date, Product, Quantity, Price, Region.  
- If the app crashes on start, try running it as Administrator.  
- Errors during file upload may mean your data file is open in another program. Close any open Excel files before uploading.  
- For large files, the app may take a few minutes to process. Wait until you see the success message.
